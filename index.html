Play Brawl Stars with Less Lag

Brawl Stars is a game that requires real-time inputs more than high-quality graphics. Higher graphics quality means higher network demands to support the game. If you're experiencing lag, you can use the HD button located in the Easyfun.gg operation bar to lower the graphics to smooth quality and improve performance.

This button works similarly to lowering video quality on streaming services like Netflix or YouTube: the lower the quality, the less buffering. In this case, lowering the graphics quality reduces the latency of game inputs.
